Spaghetti

Description

long, skinny noodles covered with meat sauce and cheese are the major elements to this delicious dish.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Meat Sauce
  • Cheeses
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Boil noodles in a pot with salted and oiled water
  2. Cook sauce in another pot
  3. Combine boiled noodles with baked sause on a plate
  4. Eat and enjoy!
