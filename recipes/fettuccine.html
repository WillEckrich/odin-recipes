Fettuccine

Description

Short, wide noodles inbetween white sauce and cheese are the major elements to this delicious dish.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • White Sauce
  • Cheeses

Steps

  1. Boil noodles in a pot
  2. Cook sauce in a different pot
  3. Combine boiled noodles and cooked sauce on a plate
  4. Eat and enjoy!
